MH370 Bathymetry and Backscatter Data

Users can download the bathymetry data over the Indian Ocean which is licensed under Creative Commons.

Data can be downloaded from the portal at no charge and there is no limit to how many requests you can place (please check the file size before you download your results).

If you need datasets in full, please contact clientservices@ga.gov.au.
